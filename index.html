About Us

The Claremont Men's Volleyball Club Team is a Division II college club that competes in regional and national tournaments. We were the reigning champions of the Southern California Collegiate Volleyball League (SCCVL) in 2015 and the runner-ups in 2016. The team is open to students from any of the Claremont Colleges (including CGU) and we pride ourselves on offering a high level of play to interested players.

We aim to provide men at the Claremont Colleges an opportunity to play volleyball in a competitive league against other colleges and universities, while still maintaining a fun, positive atmosphere. Practices are typically 2-3 times per week in the evenings or Sunday afternoons and we play beach volleyball with the women's team on Friday afternoons.

Our league season is in the Spring semester and ends with a national tournament in April, though we typically hold open-gym practices in the fall, before deciding on a roster at some point in November. Anyone is welcome to join the open practices in September and October, and once we have decided on practice times, we will post them here.
